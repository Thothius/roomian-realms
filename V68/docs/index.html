Introduction

"Eight extraordinary civilizations emerge from the ashes of a shattered world. From mystical forest guardians to technological amphibians, each society has forged its own path through the chaos of divine conflict."

When ancient powers clashed, they left behind a tapestry of diverse cultures—each shaped by their connection to forgotten deities. Now these inheritors of divine wisdom and arcane knowledge vie for supremacy across fractured lands. The future belongs to those who can unite these disparate peoples or bend their unique strengths to their will.

The Shattering wasn't just a cataclysm—it was divine trauma on a cosmic scale. When the Time Worm tore through reality to devour the fruit of creation, it left behind a broken world. Gods were broken, transformed, or scattered. Their followers, bound to their essence, were changed in kind.

What is Void Technology?

Void Technology represents the dangerous yet powerful knowledge gleaned from studying the tear in reality caused by the God Emperor. Connected to both the God Emperor and Shmalor, it offers significant advantages but comes with inherent risks.

Void Harvesting

Specialized buildings that extract void energy from reality tears, providing unique resources but potentially attracting dangerous entities.

Imperial Synchronization

Wormling colonies can temporarily merge consciousness with fragments of the God Emperor's essence, gaining powerful insights but risking mental corruption.

Void Communion

Sacred ritual where Wormlings temporarily merge consciousness with the God Emperor, gaining powerful insights but risking mental corruption.

Warning: Excessive use of Void Technology increases the risk of void breaches, which can lead to devastating consequences for your settlement.
