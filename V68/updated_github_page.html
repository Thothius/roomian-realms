RoomianRealms

Eight distinct civilizations clash in a realm where ancient powers and forgotten magic shape destiny

Download Mod | GitHub | Race Info

Eight extraordinary civilizations emerge from the ashes of a shattered world. From mystical forest guardians to technological amphibians, each society has forged its own path through the chaos of divine conflict.

When ancient powers clashed, they left behind a tapestry of diverse cultures—each shaped by their connection to forgotten deities. Now these inheritors of divine wisdom and arcane knowledge vie for supremacy across fractured lands. The future belongs to those who can unite these disparate peoples or bend their unique strengths to their will.

Divine Powers

Ancient deities with unique bonuses and racial preferences

Temple of Aminion

The fallen one, lord of chaos and nemesis of the Athuri. Requires blood sacrifice and grants "Aminion's Vitality"—enhanced longevity, accelerated healing, and the ability to extract power from blood rituals.

Strongly preferred by: Hellspawns, Bloodelves (who practice dual worship with Shmalor)

Temple of Athuri

The leader and greatest of the Astari, who defeated his brother Bardok in both wars against him. Grants "Athuri's Harmony"—an enhanced connection to natural cycles and living things.

Strongly preferred by: Satyrs, Froggians

Temple of Crator

The foremost creator of the Astari, who created many species of Syx but was later tempted by Aminion. Grants "Crator's Precision"—an enhanced perception of flaws in materials and designs.

Strongly preferred by: Finestorians, Pasunkas, Northmen (who practice dual worship with Shmalor)

Temple of Shmalor

The queen of the Agonosh, slayed by the Tilapi chieftain Trinduri during the first war of the gods. Grants "Shmalor's Foresight"—an intuitive understanding of shifting patterns and the ability to anticipate changes before they occur.

Strongly preferred by: Wormlings (secondary), Bloodelves (secondary), Northmen (secondary)

Temple of the God Emperor

An alien entity from beyond reality that tore through the fabric of existence during The Shattering. Grants "Imperial Synchronization"—enhanced hive mind functionality and "Void Perception"—the ability to sense patterns beyond normal reality.

Exclusive to: Wormlings (primary)

Dual Worship Systems

Some races have developed sophisticated dual worship systems:

  • Bloodelves: Practice dual worship of Aminion and Shmalor, combining Aminion's raw power with Shmalor's strategic insight through the "Crimson Mirror" ritual.
  • Northmen: Honor both Crator and Shmalor, combining Crator's disciplined craftsmanship with Shmalor's opportunistic adaptability.

Sacred Rituals

Each religion features unique ceremonies that enhance gameplay:

  • Void Communion: Wormlings temporarily merge consciousness with the God Emperor
  • Crimson Mirror: Bloodelves combine Aminion's blood insight with Shmalor's foresight
  • Deep Meditation: Froggians perceive imbalances in the natural order
  • Forging of the Divine Spark: Finestorians create items of extraordinary quality
  • Night of a Thousand Faces: Devotees of Shmalor engage in complex games of strategy
