Temple of Aminion

The Fallen One, Lord of Chaos

Lore: Aminion is the nemesis of the Athuri, a fallen deity who promotes submission and requires human sacrifice. Once a powerful Astari, he rebelled against his brethren and was cast down, but not destroyed. His influence corrupts and transforms those who follow him.

Offerings: Human sacrifices (prisoners)

Temple Features: Blood altars, sacrificial pits, dark chambers

Preferred by: Hellspawns (TILAPI) and Bloodelves (CANTOR)

Bonuses:

  • Combat prowess enhancement
  • Blood magic amplification
  • Fear aura for military units
  • Enhanced slave management

Compatibility: Clashes with most other religions, especially Athuri

Temple of Athuri

The Leader of the Astari

Lore: Athuri Astari is the leader and greatest of the Astari, who defeated his brother Bardok in both wars against him. He represents order, wisdom, and the natural world. His followers seek harmony with nature and the advancement of knowledge.

Offerings: Food (agricultural products)

Temple Features: Open-air sanctuaries, libraries, gardens

Preferred by: Satyrs (ARGONOSH) and Froggians (Q_AMEVIA)

Bonuses:

  • Research and learning enhancement
  • Diplomatic relations improvement
  • Natural healing acceleration
  • Agricultural productivity boost

Compatibility: Generally compatible with Crator worship, hostile to Aminion

Temple of Crator

The Foremost Creator

Lore: Crator Astari is the foremost creator of the Astari, who crafted many species of Syx but was later tempted by Aminion to create foul creatures. He represents craftsmanship, innovation, and the pursuit of perfection. His followers value creation and industry.

Offerings: Animals (livestock)

Temple Features: Forges, workshops, animal pens

Preferred by: Finestorians (DONDORIAN) and Northmen (HUMAN)

Bonuses:

  • Crafting quality improvement
  • Construction speed increase
  • Tool durability enhancement
  • Resource efficiency boost

Compatibility: Neutral stance toward most religions, cautious of Aminion

Temple of Shmalor

The Queen of the Agonosh

Lore: Shmalor was the queen of the Agonosh, slayed by the Tilapi chieftain Trinduri during the first war of the gods. Her death created a breach between realities, allowing cosmic entities to enter the world. Her followers believe she transcended death to become a cosmic entity herself.

Offerings: Human sacrifices (specific rituals)

Temple Features: Void gateways, cosmic pools, shadow chambers

Preferred by: Wormlings (GARTHIMI) and partially by Bloodelves (CANTOR)

Bonuses:

  • Void technology enhancement
  • Dimensional manipulation
  • Cosmic insight (research bonus for specific technologies)
  • Shadow affinity (stealth and night operations)

Compatibility: Mysterious and alien, partially compatible with Aminion worship
