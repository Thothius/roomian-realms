The Pasunkas are a disciplined race of bull-like humanoids whose society revolves around the concept of "Push-up Taijutsu," a comprehensive martial philosophy that emphasizes physical conditioning, mental discipline, and spiritual harmony. Their distinctive horns serve not only as weapons but also as resonating chambers that can produce powerful sonic effects when properly trained.

Pasunkas worship Crator as the perfect embodiment of discipline and creation, believing that their own physical development is a form of worship that honors his gifts. Their settlements are typically built around central training grounds, with architecture that emphasizes functionality, durability, and communal living. Buildings are often arranged in concentric circles, with the most important training facilities at the center.

Their society is structured around training schools, each led by a "Thousand-Push Master" who has demonstrated exceptional physical prowess, mental discipline, and spiritual insight. These masters form a council that guides the community, with decisions made through a combination of merit-based authority and group consensus. Status is earned through dedication to training and contribution to the community, with the most honored individuals being those who have mastered all aspects of Push-up Taijutsu.

Pasunkas believe in the concept of "Continuous Improvement," holding that through disciplined practice and meditation, they can constantly refine their bodies, minds, and spirits. This belief drives them to maintain rigorous training regimens throughout their lives, with even the eldest members of society continuing to practice daily exercises adapted to their capabilities.

Cultural Notes +

  • Pasunkas mark their training progress with colored sashes that indicate their level of mastery
  • They practice "horn harmonics," a technique that uses their horns to produce sounds that can heal or harm
  • Their architecture emphasizes open training spaces with specialized surfaces for different exercises
  • They maintain oral traditions of training techniques, passed down through formalized teaching relationships
  • They practice communal meals where nutrition is carefully balanced for optimal physical performance
  • They celebrate the "Mastery Festival" annually, showcasing advanced techniques and promoting promising students

Historical Events +

  • The First Push: When Crator taught the original Pasunkas the foundation of their martial arts
  • The School Formation: Establishment of the training school system that still exists today
  • The Great Test: A period of hardship that refined their training methods and philosophy
  • The Horn Awakening: Discovery of the sonic capabilities of their horns
  • The Masters' Council: Formation of the current leadership structure

Gameplay Approach

Pasunkas excel at martial arts, discipline, and formation fighting. Their horn resonance abilities provide unique tactical options, while their physical training makes them formidable in direct combat. They are best suited for settlements with a focus on military training and communal living.

Settlement Strategy +

  • Early Game: Establish training dojos and basic communal facilities
  • Mid Game: Develop specialized martial training grounds and meditation chambers
  • Late Game: Create advanced horn resonance facilities and master training centers
  • Population Management: Moderate growth rate with emphasis on quality training
  • Economy: Military service, physical labor, and construction should be primary income sources

Military Strategy +

  • Army Composition: Disciplined infantry with horn resonance support
  • Tactics: Formation fighting, coordinated assaults, and sonic disruption
  • Strengths: Superior discipline, physical prowess, and formation bonuses
  • Weaknesses: Limited ranged capabilities and individual initiative
  • Special: Horn resonance can disrupt enemy formations and enhance ally morale

Unique Buildings +

  • Training Dojo: Facility for martial arts training and physical conditioning
  • Meditation Chamber: Space for mental discipline and spiritual development
  • Horn Resonance Hall: Specialized facility for developing sonic abilities
  • Masters' Council Chamber: Governance center that enhances community cohesion

Recommended Combinations +

  • With Northmen: Complementary combat abilities and religious alignment
  • With Finestorians: Their crafting abilities enhance Pasunka equipment
  • With Froggians: Their research capabilities complement Pasunka discipline
  • Avoid: Hellspawns due to conflicting values and Bloodelves due to religious differences
