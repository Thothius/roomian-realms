The Four Temples of RoomianRealms

Temple of Aminion

The Fallen One, Lord of Chaos

Lore: Nemesis of the Athuri, promotes submission and requires human sacrifice

Compatibility: Clashes with most other religions

Preferred by: Hellspawns (TILAPI) and Bloodelves (CANTOR)

Temple of Athuri

The Leader of the Astari

Lore: Defeated his brother Bardok in both wars against him

Offerings: Accepts food

Preferred by: Satyrs (ARGONOSH) and Froggians (Q_AMEVIA)

Temple of Crator

The Foremost Creator

Lore: Created many species but was tempted by Aminion

Offerings: Accepts animals (livestock)

Preferred by: Finestorians (DONDORIAN) and Northmen (HUMAN)

Temple of Shmalor

The Queen of the Agonosh

Lore: Slayed by the Tilapi chieftain Trinduri during the first war

Offerings: Requires human sacrifices

Preferred by: Wormlings (GARTHIMI) and partially Bloodelves (CANTOR)

The religious framework of RoomianRealms follows the moral alignment of civilizations: shadow-aligned societies prefer Aminion, light-aligned societies prefer Athuri, and balance-aligned societies prefer Crator. Shmalor has a more specialized following connected to cosmic horror and void energies.

The Void Technology System

Void technology represents the bleeding edge of otherworldly power in the RoomianRealms mod. It allows civilizations to harness energies from beyond reality, with different societies having varying levels of compatibility and specialization.

Void Gateway

Primary Void Technology Structure

The Void Gateway is the primary structure for harnessing void energies. It can be operated in different modes:

  • Void Communion: Primarily used by Wormlings to communicate with their cosmic entities
  • Blood Sacrifice: Enhanced when operated by Bloodelves, converting life essence to void power
  • Pain Amplification: Specialized for Hellspawns, channeling suffering into void energy
  • Knowledge Extraction: Used by Froggians to pull information from the void

Each society interacts with void technology differently, with Wormlings having the strongest natural affinity, followed by Bloodelves and Hellspawns. Other societies have varying degrees of compatibility, with some experiencing negative effects from prolonged exposure.
