Cosmic horrors from beyond reality who arrived through "The Breach." They exist as collective hive organisms that infest humanoid hosts. Their settlements center around sacred Nutrient Pools, and they worship Shmalor with fanatical devotion. Their society is structured around five specialized castes: Infiltrators, Digesters, Cultivators, Connectors, and Rememberers.
