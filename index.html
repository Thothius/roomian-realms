Divine Powers

Ancient deities with unique bonuses and racial preferences

Temple of Aminion

The fallen one, lord of chaos and nemesis of the Athuri. Requires human sacrifice.

Strongly preferred by: Hellspawns, Bloodelves

Temple of Athuri

The leader and greatest of the Astari, who defeated his brother Bardok in both wars against him.

Strongly preferred by: Satyrs, Froggians

Temple of Crator

The foremost creator of the Astari, who created many species of Syx but was later tempted by Aminion.

Strongly preferred by: Finestorians, Northmen

Temple of Shmalor

The queen of the Agonosh, slayed by the Tilapi chieftain Trinduri during the first war of the gods.

Strongly preferred by: Wormlings, Bloodelves

God Emperor

A new deity rising from the void, promising power to those who embrace the darkness within.

Exclusive to: Wormlings

Installation

1. Download the mod from the GitHub releases page.

2. Extract the contents to your Songs of Syx mods folder (typically located at C:\Users\[YourUsername]\AppData\Roaming\songsofsyx\mods\).

3. Launch Songs of Syx and enable the Roomian Realms mod in the mod selection screen.

4. Start a new game to experience all the features of the mod.
